Beta Testing Agreement

Garmin Integration - EarthRhythms Mobile

Effective as of April 19, 2026

1. Agreement Overview

This Beta Testing Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between EarthRhythms ("Company"), a subsidiary of Quintessential Cloud Systems (QCS), and you, the user ("Tester"), for the purpose of testing the Garmin wearable integration feature within the EarthRhythms Mobile application (the "Beta Feature"). By participating in this beta testing program, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined herein.

2. Beta Testing Scope

The Beta Feature includes:

  • OAuth-based connection to Garmin wearable devices
  • Real-time synchronization of sleep, heart rate, stress, and activity metrics
  • Integration of wearable data into the EarthRhythms circadian rhythm analysis
  • Display of personalized insights based on wearable-derived metrics
  • Automated daily data collection from Garmin Health API

3. Data Collection and Usage

As a beta tester, you acknowledge that the Company will collect and process the following data:

  • Wearable Metrics: Sleep patterns, heart rate, HRV, stress levels, respiration, body battery, activity metrics, and steps
  • OAuth Tokens: Temporary access and refresh tokens to retrieve data from Garmin Health API
  • Usage Data: App interaction logs, feature usage patterns, and timing of data syncs
  • Error Logs: Technical errors, API failures, and system performance metrics
  • User Profile: Email, location preferences, and account metadata
⚠️ Important: Your wearable data will be stored on secure servers and used solely for improving the Beta Feature. The Company will never sell or share this data with third parties without your explicit consent.

4. Privacy and Data Security

The Company is committed to protecting your privacy. All wearable data will be:

  • Encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Stored in secure databases with role-based access control
  • Subject to the EarthRhythms Privacy Policy (available at info.qcloud.systems/privacy-policy.html)
  • Retained only for the duration of the beta testing program plus 30 days after termination
  • Deleted upon your written request at any time

5. Tester Responsibilities

As a beta tester, you agree to:

  • Provide honest, constructive feedback on the Beta Feature
  • Report bugs, crashes, and unexpected behavior promptly via the feedback system
  • Test the feature regularly during the beta testing period
  • Maintain the confidentiality of any pre-release information shared with you
  • Not attempt to reverse-engineer, decompile, or modify the Beta Feature
  • Not share beta access credentials or early access with unauthorized parties
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

6. Company Responsibilities

The Company agrees to:

  • Maintain reasonable efforts to secure your data against unauthorized access
  • Respond to critical security issues within 24 hours
  • Provide technical support for beta-related issues via email or in-app messaging
  • Implement your feedback where feasible and provide updates on enhancements
  • Transparently communicate known issues and limitations
